# One Day Outing in Bengaluru

## Places to Visit

1. **ISKCON Temple**
   - A magnificent temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, known for its exquisite architecture.
2. **Lalbagh Botanical Garden**
   - Famous for its diverse flora, the garden features a stunning glass house.
3. **Cubbon Park**
   - A lush green park, perfect for a peaceful stroll or picnic.
4. **Vidhana Soudha**
   - An impressive building housing the state legislature of Karnataka.
5. **Nandi Hills**
   - A scenic hill station ideal for sunrise viewing and trekking.

## Foods to Try

### Breakfast:

- **Vegetarian:** Idli and vada with sambar at MTR (Mavalli Tiffin Rooms).
- **Non-Vegetarian:** dosa at Brahmin's Coffee Bar.

### Lunch:

- **Vegetarian:** Thali at Nagarathnamma Tiffin Room in VV Puram Food Street.
- **Non-Vegetarian:** Chicken biryani at Paradise Biryani.

### Dinner:

- **Vegetarian:** Paneer butter masala with naan at Punjab Grill.
- **Non-Vegetarian:** Fish curry with rice at Karavalli.
